Bark & Blade Privacy Policy

At Bark & Blade, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with the utmost care and transparency. This Privacy Policy outlines in comprehensive detail how we collect, use, process, store, and share your information when you interact with our website, services, and community platforms. We understand the importance of your trust and strive to be clear about our practices, ensuring you are fully informed about your rights and how your data is managed in alignment with global data protection regulations. Our dedication to fostering a secure and respectful environment extends beyond the art of wood carving to the digital space where our community thrives. We encourage you to read this policy thoroughly to understand our approach to privacy, which is an integral part of our commitment to you, our valued carvers and creators. This document serves as a living testament to our promise of safeguarding your digital footprint while you explore, learn, and connect with the world of woodcraft. Your journey from grain to greatness should be free from privacy concerns, allowing you to focus entirely on your artistic endeavors and community engagement. We regularly review and update our practices to ensure they meet the highest standards of data protection and privacy, reflecting our ongoing commitment to your security and peace of mind.

This policy applies to all visitors, users, and others who access or use the Bark & Blade website and its associated services, including our Craft Library, Workshops & Tutorials, The Artisan Shop, and the Global Community Hub. By accessing or using any part of Bark & Blade, you signify your understanding and agreement to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain from using our services. We have structured this policy to be as accessible and understandable as possible, breaking down complex legal requirements into clear, actionable points. Our goal is to empower you with knowledge about your data, enabling you to make informed decisions about your privacy settings and interactions within our platform. We are committed to maintaining a secure and trustworthy environment where your personal information is protected and respected at all times. This commitment is foundational to building a thriving and engaged community where creativity and craftsmanship can flourish without compromise. We believe that a strong privacy framework is essential for fostering innovation and trust in the digital age, and we are dedicated to upholding these principles in every aspect of our operations.

1. Information We Collect: Account Registration Data

When you register for a Bark & Blade account, we collect essential personal information necessary to establish and manage your profile. This typically includes your full name, email address, and a secure password. We may also request a username, which will be visible to other community members in the Global Community Hub. This data is fundamental for identifying you as a unique user, enabling personalized experiences within My Workshop Dashboard, and facilitating secure access to exclusive content and features. The collection of this information ensures that we can provide you with tailored services, communicate effectively regarding your account, and maintain the integrity of our user base. We prioritize the security of this foundational data, employing robust encryption and access controls to protect it from unauthorized access or misuse.

2. Information We Collect: Transactional Data

When you make purchases through The Artisan Shop, we collect information related to your transactions. This includes details such as the products you buy, the quantity, pricing, and the date and time of your purchase. While we process payments through secure third-party payment gateways, we do not directly store your credit card or sensitive financial information on our servers. We receive confirmation of successful payments and may retain partial payment details (e.g., last four digits of a card number) for transaction verification and customer support purposes. This data is crucial for fulfilling your orders, managing returns, and providing effective customer service, ensuring a smooth and reliable shopping experience within our platform.

3. Information We Collect: Shipping and Billing Details

To process and deliver your orders from The Artisan Shop, we collect necessary shipping and billing information. This typically includes your shipping address, billing address, phone number, and any specific delivery instructions you provide. This information is solely used for the purpose of order fulfillment, ensuring that your tools and materials reach you accurately and efficiently. We share this data with our trusted shipping partners only to the extent necessary for delivery. We maintain strict protocols to protect this sensitive information, ensuring it is handled with care and used only for its intended purpose of completing your purchases.

4. Information We Collect: User-Generated Content

In the Global Community Hub, you have the opportunity to upload and share your creations, participate in forums, and engage with other members. Any content you voluntarily submit, such as photos of your carvings, text posts, comments, or messages, is considered user-generated content. We collect and store this content to facilitate community interaction, showcase talent in the Community Gallery, and enable participation in challenges and contests. Please be mindful that information you post publicly may be viewed and accessed by others. We encourage you to exercise discretion when sharing personal details within public forums.

5. Information We Collect: Technical and Usage Data

When you access and use our website, we automatically collect certain technical and usage data. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, access times, and referring website addresses. We also gather information about your interactions with the site, such as pages visited, features used, and the duration of your sessions. This data helps us understand how our services are being used, identify areas for improvement, and optimize the overall user experience. 6. Information We Collect: Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Bark & Blade utilizes cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather insights into website usage. We use both session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period. You have the option to manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of some website features.
